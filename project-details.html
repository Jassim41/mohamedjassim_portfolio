IoT Based Water Management Model Using Machine Learning Approaches for Indoor Gardening

Project information

  • Category: Internet of Things, Machine Learning
  • Project date: 01 December, 2024

Water, the life-giving force, animates and sustains all living organisms. As the human population continues to grow exponentially, the demand for water resources is escalating, highlighting the urgent need for conservation. Pothos, an adaptable and resilient plant, thrives in various environments, including the ability to thrive in waterbased systems, making it a potential ally in water conservation efforts. Indoor gardening brings the beauty of nature indoors, allowing year-round gardening and adding aesthetic and health benefits to living spaces. Soil moisture control, pH monitoring, water management challenges are addressed in water management model in indoor gardening. With a focus on optimizing growth for Pothos plants popular for indoor gardening and renowned for its ability to purify indoor air through oxygen release. The primary objective is to create an economical and effective solution. To achieve this objective this proposed innovative IoT- based water management model using Machine learning approach for indoor gardening. The key findings of this proposed work is to develop a recommendation model for water management for indoor gardening. ML is utilized for the continuous monitoring and regulation of essential water parameters crucial for the optimal growth of Pothos plants. The proposed model delivers precise, real-time data using sensors on water pH and soil moisture levels, eliminating the need for manual data collection. This proposed work incorporates cutting-edge sensors, microcontrollers, and communication modules, all coordinated through a cloud-based data storage platform. Rigorous testing in a real-world setting thoroughly evaluates the functionality, reliability, and accuracy of the ML-driven water management prototype. Emphasizing sustainability, the system optimizes resource utilization, diminishes chemical inputs, and fosters soil health. This ML-based water management model for Pothos plants offers a promising solution to uplift indoor gardening practices. With the potential to increase oxygen, conserve water, and promote sustainability, this system signifies a significant stride toward a more efficient and environmentally conscious indoor agriculture industry.

Smart Accident Caution System

Project information

  • Category: Internet of Things, Embedded C
  • Project date: 28 February, 2023

India faces a critical issue with road accidents, resulting in a significant loss of life and property. Road accidents are a major cause of death and injury in India, with thousands of lives lost each year. These accidents are often caused by factors such as poor road conditions, reckless driving, and adverse weather conditions. This project addresses this challenge by developing a "Smart Accident Caution System" that utilizes IoT technology to proactively prevent accidents. The system employs ultrasonic distance sensors and servo motors to implement a multi-level warning system that alerts drivers as they approach danger zones, such as landslides, poorly constructed roads, and flood-prone areas. By strategically deploying this system in high-risk locations, we aim to significantly enhance road safety, minimize fatalities, and reduce property damage.

  • Project date: 15 June, 2023

In today's space exploration landscape, SpaceX's innovations have redefined industry standards. This project aims to predict the outcome of Falcon 9 rocket first stage landings using data analytics and web scraping. By analyzing SpaceX's website data, including launch schedules and historical mission details, predictive models will be developed to forecast landing success probabilities. The goal is to provide stakeholders with actionable insights for cost optimization and strategic decisionmaking. Given the significant cost savings associated with successful first stage landings, accurately predicting these outcomes is crucial for market competitiveness. Through this endeavour, the project seeks to foster innovation and healthy competition within the space launch industry. College Core Book Management

Project information

  • Category: Web Design, PHP, MySQL
  • Project date: 1 April, 2022

This project focuses on creating a user-friendly bookstore management system specifically designed for students at Alagappa College of Agriculture (AGAC). The primary objective is to provide convenient access to books for students across all departments, encompassing both Arts and Science divisions. The system incorporates a division-based structure, allowing Arts students to access books relevant to their department, while Science students have access to books within their respective departments. The Science division further categorizes books by department, year (both PG and UG), and semester. Each semester is associated with various subjects, ensuring that books are organized in a clear and accessible manner. This structured approach facilitates easy book retrieval for students from all departments. The system integrates a robust login system that requires students to authenticate using their department name and code. This ensures secure access to the system and its resources. This project aims to enhance the reading experience for AGAC students by providing a centralized and efficient platform for accessing a wide range of books.
